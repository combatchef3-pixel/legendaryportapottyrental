Frequently Asked Questions

How many portable toilets do I need for my event?

As a general rule for events in the United States, you should have one unit for every 50 guests for a 4-hour event. If alcohol is being served or the event lasts longer, we recommend increasing that number by 15-20% to maintain hygiene.

What is the average price for porta potty rental for a weekend?

Does a porta potty rental service include cleaning?

Yes, our portable toilet rental service includes a standard weekly cleaning for long-term rentals. This involves waste extraction, interior sanitization, and restocking of toilet paper and hand sanitizer.

Can I place a portable bathroom rental anywhere?

Units must be placed on level, sturdy ground with clear access for our service trucks. Our team will help you choose the best spot in your local area to ensure the unit remains upright and accessible for pumping.

Are there ADA-compliant portable restrooms for events?

Absolutely. We provide ADA-compliant units nationwide that feature wider doors, interior grab bars, and enough space for a wheelchair to turn, ensuring your event in Boston or Denver is inclusive.

Clarendon, AR Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Clarendon is a city in, and the county seat of, Monroe County, Arkansas, United States. Located in the Arkansas Delta, the city's position on the White River at the mouth of the Cache River has defined the community since first incorporating in 1859. Although the river has brought devastation and disaster to the city occasionally throughout history, it has also provided economic opportunities, transportation, recreation and tourism to the city.
